Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

What Data Is Collected

Overlord Bot accesses data from the following Google services, solely for the authorized Google account (chaosoverlordbot@gmail.com):

  • Gmail -- email messages (read, send, and manage)
  • Google Docs -- document content (create, read, and modify)
  • Google Slides -- presentation content (create and modify)
  • Google Sheets -- spreadsheet content (create, read, and modify)
  • Google Calendar -- calendar events (read and manage)

No data from other Google accounts or other users is accessed.

How Data Is Used

Data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Processing incoming email messages and sending replies
  • Creating and editing documents, presentations, and spreadsheets as part of automated workflows
  • Reading and managing calendar events for scheduling purposes

Data is not used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than the automated workflow tasks described above.

Data Sharing

Data accessed by Overlord Bot is not shared with any third parties. The bot operates as a personal tool for its operator and does not transmit user data to external services beyond what is necessary for its core functions (e.g., sending email replies via Gmail).

Data Storage

Overlord Bot does not persistently store email content, document content, or calendar data beyond what is needed for immediate processing. Transient data (such as message metadata for threading) may be held in memory during processing and is discarded afterward.

Data Deletion

Since no persistent copies of Google user data are stored, there is no accumulated data to delete. Revoking the application's access through Google Account permissions will immediately stop all access.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact chaoseternal@gmail.com.
